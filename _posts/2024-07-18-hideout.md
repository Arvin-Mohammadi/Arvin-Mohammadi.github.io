This Manga was only 9 chapters but oh my god was it an emotional roller coaster. The art was absolutely out of this world as if it was fueled by nightmares. It's a really good read and the story will make you feel dread, rage, pity and many other emotions. As a horror experiences I can safely say it is one of the best experiences I've had. And despite the beautifully disgusting art style, I count this as psychological horror. 

> How long was I locked up in that room? A day? A week? I could no longer tell. On top of driving you crazy, the darkness and isolation also robbed you of all your sense of time.

Honestly I don't think the plot or the characters were the highlight of this manga ... rather the sheer nightmarish scenes and the art style. The insanely high-level art really carried the whole thing. The plot was ... interesting ... but that's about it. The characters I did not feel any connection to. 

![Hideout](assets/img/posts/2024-07-18/3.jpg)

> Score - 9/10
