**I went out for a late night drive with my best friend possibly for the last time in this country** as he's to leave the country in about 2 months or so and I'm leaving tomorrow to the Capital. The experience was ... surreal to say the least. We talked, laughed, ate some shit food, watched some Markiplier ... and then went for a drive around the city and some "music".

**I think it's safe to say that we drove around the entire city one and a half times with my select songs.** Two Triple-S Tier albums, one was "World on Fire by Myles Kennedy and Slash" and the other was "Fallen by Evanescence" and holy fuck I've never experienced something like that. I was in a car moving at 150 KPH with the volume on MAX for more than an hour. It was so fun. And if the universe doesn't decide to fuck us, maybe we'll get to try that again in some other place.

**I realized something** as the wind was hitting my hand violently ... I honestly enjoy living even with all of the shit life throws at me. I know I have to take it easy and just let myself live a free life. Free of stress. Free of care. I know I'm smart enough to figure out a solution to my own problems. I have to trust myself. I also have to trust the universe just a little bit more, cause despite the universe wanting to fuck you over at all times ... there is almost always a way to unfuck things.

**Also I've got something to say about the people in my life.** I know that there are a bunch of people whom I care for and a bunch of people who care for me. But for this friend group ... there is something different about them than all of the other people in my life. And I know what it is. They are the only people who make me feel like I'm at home. Thanks to them I know what "home" feels like ... I never knew it before them. Thanks to them I know that the feeling exists in this world and it's not from a fantasy land.
