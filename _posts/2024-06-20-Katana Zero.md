_This scene captured everything I love about gaming_

This game ... This fucking game ... I don't even know how to start. Do I talk about the perfect animations? The perfect sound design? The perfect music? The perfect story? The perfect character writing? The perfect level design? The perfect DIALOGUE SYSTEM EVEN? Where the fuck should I even start. I can not get over how insanely well-made this game was. It was one the most enjoyable experiences I've had playing it ... 

Let's start where the game starts. The protagonist is a killer. His job is to do the dirty work for his higher-ups. Instructions given, instructions carried out, no questions asked. What I absolutely adored about this game was that EVERY little detail counted. from the Neon Sign of the "Murdower Hotel" that flickered into "Murder Hotel" to the fact our protagonist blasted techno music in his earphones while "doing his job", I loved that every musical piece was a fucking banger and I would totally listen to his playlist while murdering multiple people. Ok you know what? Let's take a listen to one of them:

{% include embed/audio.html src='assets/music/posts/2024-06-20/ludoWic - Katana ZERO (Katana ZERO OST).mp3' title='LudoWic - Katana ZERO' %}

I loved every single boss fight I loved every single level I loved everything about this fucking game ... everything. But ... The Single ... Most ... Adorable thing ... In All Gaming History ... is that little girl from his neighbor apartment. I simply adored the interactions those two had. A serial killer ... a professional assassin ... at the mercy of a little girl with her toys and her sarcasm. I haven't felt anything quite like this before. I'm not even slightly exaggerating when I say I found myself playing through every level just in the hope of seeing her at the end of that day. There are theories that the girl wasn't real. That she was the last remaining pieces of Zero's morality. I don't want to believe that. I don't.
