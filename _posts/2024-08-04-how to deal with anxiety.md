## Don't Forget the Basics 

Well to be honest one of the things that I often forget is the simple fact that sleeping adjusts the hormons and restarts the body. And like all feelings, anxiety stems from the hormons. A healthy mind needs a healthy vessel right? Sleep deprevation can easily lead to difficulties with controling the anxiety. So if you're anxious for seemingly on reason, chances are you lack good sleep.

## One Method to Rule Them All

LISTEN! JUST FREAKING LISTEN! Anxiety is your friend and it's always trying to remind you something that's very important to you. Try to work with your anxiety rather than trying to kill it or escape from it. This might seem difficult specially when you're having an anxiety attack or are simply overwhelmed by the emotion... But there is nothing to be afraid of. You shouldn't let the anxiety become a permanent feeling that controls your actions and feelings. No. You should listen to what it has to say and act accordingly. Don't let it get counter-productive. Just remember that you are the one in control here. 

> Consistency is king, and listening to your anxiety is key to consistency.
