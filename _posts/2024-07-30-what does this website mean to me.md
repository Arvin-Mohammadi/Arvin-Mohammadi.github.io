Hello my dear friend! Once again I'm writing for you and I know you're not reading any of this. Funny feeling that. Today I'm trying to answer a simple question: What does this website mean to me? And just by the way, I nicked the idea for this format of diary from Barney Stinson in HIMYM when he was updating his blog for his followers, writing about how awsome he is, right after having sex with some random girl. 😆

## First Attempt

**Well I'm just doing it for the attention.** When I started this new format of writing my diary that was the first thought I had.

> Oh! I'm just doing this because like everything else I do, I want to show it to others, getting some sort of praise out of it. I'm doing it because I'm an attention whore and in need of constant validation from others

That thought drove me to not tell about this project to anyone, even to my best friend. I've mentioned some form of new diary I'm trying and I'm loving it, but never mentioned what it is and never gave a clue for him how to find it. There is the fact that I'm putting it on public domain but maybe that is a hint that I'm an exhibitionist rather than an attention whore 😂😭 Oh god! If you're up there, forgive me. So that sorts that out. No need for attention or validation is involved now that no one knows about it. 

## Second Attempt 

**I'm trying to preserve my own life.** Maybe this is a way for me to remember my life when I reach the end of it. It's a way to evaluate how I've been living and to remember what I cared about. And that may be true with all the effort I'm putting into preserving my data, all of the pictures and my galleries, all of the movies and the experiences I've enjoyed throughout time... So I think that's partially true. When I preserve data this thoroughly, I can trace back a lot of my own emotions, thoughts, and experiences. But still I don't think that's the entirety of it. I can't do something just for the end result of it and keep up with it for so long. And I have been searching for the perfect method of storing data for so long and now that I've found it I'm enjoying every second of the process. That means there is more to it than "how useful this process will be in time". Why am I enjoying this so much? 

![Omori](assets/img/posts/2024-07-30/2.png)

## Third Attempt

**Right now I think that I'm very close to the true answer.** Let's just put the fact that I have OCD aside for a second (because that's one of the main reasons why I like categorizing data so much). I've been updating this website for a couple of months and had some time to think about the initial question. I enjoy the fact that whenever I'm posting something here, it feels like I'm unfolding a new secret about myself. Each post feels like finding the answer to a math problem that I've been obsessing with, for multiple years.

**I'm obseseed with understanding myself.** That's the most important thing for me. Because ever since I was a kid I was struggling to understand how I operate, and as a result, most of what I did brought me disenjoyment. The more I understood myself, the more I like my social interactions, daily life, and experiences. I can appreciate what I have better and I can have more control over myself and my bahaviors. Even now I re-read most of my posts in order to understand why I'm feeling a certain way and to remember how I've dealt with similar situations before. 

> To me this website is the culmination of all my efforts in order to understand myself
