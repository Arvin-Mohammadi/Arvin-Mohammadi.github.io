## Sleep Paralysis is Fucking Scary Man!

**I had another episode of sleep paralysis last night which indicates that my sleep schedule is absolutely fucked** and I don't know how to fix it. I've been trying to for the past god knows how long. This time, I didn't feel anyone creeping their hand on my feet or my tap shoulders or see a figure above my head like the times before; This episode was somehow more terrifying than all of the previous episodes combined. 

## What I Experienced ...

**Keep in mind that I'm sleeping in my bed but when I have the sensation of waking up I find myself leaning on the wall of dorm bathroom.** I was standing ... and seeing the bathroom floor with my own eyes, except that it felt like the bathroom was from an evil parallel universe. The colors were no longer white, it was a dirty yellow and brown and there was very small creatures moving across the floor towards me. I closed my eyes and re-opened them in the hope of getting out of there only to see even a bigger swarm of ... small insect type things running towards me. I closed my eyes on more time and then re-opened them ... another scene was taking shape in front of me 

**Before I could find out what the scene was, I heard a voice that distantly said "Arvin you good?".** It was my roommate trying to wake me up. I just fell out of the episode and that was the end of it. The thing that terrifies me the most were not the insects or the evil parallel universe aethetic of the scene. It's the fact that my sense of space-time was completely shifted and I honestly felt like I was physically standing on my two feet and leaning on the wall instead of lying on the bed. I have no idea how that works.
