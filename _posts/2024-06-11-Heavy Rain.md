**This game is one of the first story-driven games I've ever played**, and for me, it was an incredibly enjoyable experience despite the story having more holes than a fucking Swiss cheese. One of my favorite moments was when Jayden and Blake break into an apartment. Jayden says, "I'm not sure this is entirely legal," and Blake responds, "Call the cops."

**There is not much else to say about the game**. The protagonist, Ethan, going through all of that pain and suffering in order to save his second kid when he couldn't save his first ... Honestly it just did something for me. The part where he had to sever his finger was one of the most intense moments I've ever experienced in gaming. However the Origami Killer confontation wasn't exactly the best ... since his motivation was somewhat shallow and unrelatable. But all and all it was a good experience. I'm glad I played this game and it always brings back memories of a simpler time. 

> Score - 8/10
