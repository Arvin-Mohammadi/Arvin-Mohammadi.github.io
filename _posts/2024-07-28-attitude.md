Hey my dear non-existing friend! You know you're slowly becoming my favorite person. Anyways, pleasantries aside, tonight I wanted to talk about **ATTITUDE!!!** Do you know why attitude is important? Because it determines how you approach **EVERYTHING** in life. 

## What's My Attitude Like? 

**My attitude sucks 😭.** I think that sentence completely wraps everything up. I always have a losing attitude, complain, and talk myself down. Where does this horrible attitude come from? My dear friend, do you remember when I talked about fear of living? ... No? You haven't been paying attention which hurts a little 😆. I think I'm afraid of having a winning mentality, or even worse, I'm afraid of winning itself, much like everything else in my life. It's easier to be passive. It's hurts less. It requires less focus. It's just easire all around. **But that's not how I want to be, you know.** I like the idea of having a charisma like Makise Kurisu. Always in the right and really hard-headed. Always smiling. I actually think that I have it in me.

## How Should I Behave Ideally?

**Rule #1: Always smile.** As Alastor puts it:

> Just because you see a smile, don’t think you know what’s going on underneath. A smile is a valuable tool, my dear! It inspires your friends, keeps your enemies guessing, and ensures that no matter what comes your way, you’re the one in control.

I honestly don't have anything else to add to that. It's just the perfect reasoning. You always have to keep smiling even trying to fool yourself into believing that you are the one in control. For me smiling is not something I can do all the time, but maybe with practice ... Even when I have a shit ton of problems heading my way ... 

**Rule #2: Always be unapologetic.** There are two sides to this coin. Firstly paired with the smile this sort of behaviour conveys to others that you are in control of what's happening. They might not like it, but in the end you'll probably be better off pissing a few people off rather than people-please. The other side of it is that when you hurt someone close to you and you actually apologize ... They know that you mean it. 

**Rule #3: Always be optimistic.** Despite being prepared for the worst, I have to be sure that the best will happen.

**Rule #4: Never let your fear control you.** The instant I stop moving forward, I'll lose everything.

> Someday ... I wanna be able to look at myself and smile with confidence.
