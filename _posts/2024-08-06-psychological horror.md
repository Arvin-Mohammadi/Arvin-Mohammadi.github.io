So ... my favorite genre in any form of media ... why? What do I love about this genre so freaking much? Do I just like to see suffering of others or do I get some sort of sick pleasure out of watching a person facing an un-imaginable horror and having multiple emotional breakdowns? Today's topic is really light-hearted and cute so let's just dive in. 😊

## What Do I Get Out of Watching Other's Suffering? 

Well the answer is ... knowledge. The simple truth is that I'm so obsessively fascinated by "fear" and what it means to me. What does it mean to TRUELY be afraid of something? I think it stems from my own lack of courage. I'm not a brave person, by any standard. I don't like being in _icky-sticky_ situations. More so, if you remember my other post which was titled "fear of everything" or something dumb like that, you would know that I'm scared shit-less of living a life. I practically torture myself everyday into believing that the world is set against me and all I tempt to do is doomed to inevitable failure, even for tasks as simple as enjoying a good video game. Me not having the courage to move forward, not having the courage to face any of this world's reality, ended up with me admiring those who do have that courage, and also being fascinated with witnessing the fear in eyes of other souls (totally not what a serial killer would say). I want to see what level of fear is required for someone to go completely insane. But even better than that is that _edge_... if something is so insanely horrific that it makes a person immediately go insane, then that's no fun. Imagine this: 

you are looking up at the sky one morning, the sky seems somewhat darker than usual ... like someone has dimmed the sun ... wait ... where is the sun? suddenly the entire sky goes dark and then becomes bright again and that's where you notice what you're looking at is a giant blue pupile ... sky went dark when the eye blinked. on the edge of horizon you notice the tentacles of this ... creature. 

Do you see what I mean? you witness something like that and you're immediately gone... Like ... your brain can't comprehend this level of threat because what are you supposed to do? run? hide? attack it? pray to it? pray to your god? what could possibly save you at that moment? Seeing that is fun and all but what I don't like is that the threat level is in-comprehendable. I want the people subjected to the fear understand what they are experiencing. I want them to understand what they're going through. What would someone do if they're so scared and so desperate and so ... what's the word god ... consumed by fear ... yeah ... what's the reaction of someone to so much fear that it's just nearly enough to make one go insane but not quite. People in this situations would do so many interesting things. Some just give up and become numb. Some let the fear consume them and let it guide their actions and do any horrible thing to just survive (it doesn't have to be a literal survival situation. for example if someone has a huge secret that's eating them from inside out...)

That's the entire thing.
