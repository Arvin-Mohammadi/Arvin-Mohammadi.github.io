FRIENDS! THANK YOU ALL FOR GATHERING HERE TO CELEBRATE ANOTHER SSS-TIER CREATION! A GIFT FROM THE HEAVENS! IT'S THE HOLY SMOKES MACHINE WHICH KEEPS US WARM IN THIS NEVER-ENDING WINTER! \**clears throat*\* As you might be able to tell, I'm a bit excited for this game because it's easily my favorite strategy game of all time.

**It's not just about the visuals**, which are gorgeous by the way, it's about the perfect harmony, balance, and imaginary realism of the human behavior in this game. The way the ruler (you) controls and manipulate the masses and the way that the masses react to everything is just so insanely beautifully rendered. Non of the things depicted in the game has ever happened and yet I can easily picture the exact same thing happening in real life with real humans. The way that humans turn against each other in the most dire situtations, the jealousy, the hate, the expectation... all of it feel too real to ignore. 

**About the techniqual side of the game** I should say that the level design in this game is right on the limit of what's achievable as *perfection* in the hands of mortals. The almost-obsessive attention to details that don't even matter is what makes this game what it is. The story is appealing and the way that the game plays out makes you feel attached to the NPCs and care for them. Whenever a NPC lost their limbs at work or died from the cold I cared. The game mechanics were really satisfying as well and the visuals... well they speak for themselves, one picture better than a thousand words, right? 

> Score: 10/10
